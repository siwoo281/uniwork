# UniWork (International Student Job Partner)

UniWork is a comprehensive web application designed to assist international students in Korea (specifically Pai Chai University students) with navigating part-time job regulations, visa requirements, and daily life.

## Key Features

### 1. Visa Regulations Guide (Rules Guide)
- **Time Limits:** Clear explanation of working hour limits for D-2 (Degree) and D-4 (Language) visas, including specific benefits for Pai Chai University students (IEQAS certified).
- **Illegal Jobs:** List of prohibited occupations (Delivery, Construction, Manufacturing, Private Tutoring) to ensure student safety.
- **Work Permit Guide:** Step-by-step instructions on applying for the "Part-time Work Permit" (ì²´ë¥˜ìê²©ì™¸ í™œë™í—ˆê°€), including required documents and procedures.
- **Visa & Travel:** Information regarding visa expiry, re-entry rules, and D-10 Job Seeker visa after graduation.
- **Tax & Insurance:** Detailed guide on the 3.3% freelancer tax, 4 Major Insurances, and tax refunds.

### 2. Visa Checker & Salary Calculator
- **Eligibility Checker:**
  - Instantly checks work eligibility based on Visa Type (D-2/D-4), TOPIK Level, GPA, and Freshman status.
  - **Freshman Exemption:** Specific logic to exempt first-semester students from GPA requirements.
  - **D-4 Residence Rule:** Checks the 6-month residence requirement for language students.
- **Salary Calculator:**
  - Estimates monthly income based on hourly wage and weekly hours.
  - **Minimum Wage Check:** Alerts users if the input wage is below the 2025 Legal Minimum Wage (10,030 KRW).
  - **Holiday Pay:** Automatically calculates expected Weekly Holiday Pay (ì£¼íœ´ìˆ˜ë‹¹) if working over 15 hours/week.

### 3. Job Hunting Support
- **Job Portals:** Direct access to popular Korean job search platforms (AlbaHeaven, Albamon) with search tips ("Foreigner Possible", "No Experience").
- **1-Minute Resume:** Generates a polite Korean text message template for job applications. Users input their details (Name, Age, Visa, TOPIK) and get a ready-to-send message.
- **Korean Phrases:** Useful Korean phrases for interviews, working hours, and emergency situations (unpaid wages, etc.).

### 4. Life & Safety Information
- **Living Guide:**
  - Housing tips (Apps vs. Real Estate Agents) and Dormitory price estimates.
  - **Essential Apps:** Recommended apps for life in Korea (Delivery, Transport, Payment).
  - **Trash Separation:** Guide on how to separate General, Food, and Recyclable waste.
  - **Medical:** Where to buy medicine (Convenience store vs. Pharmacy).
- **Safety:**
  - Emergency contacts (Police, Fire, Immigration).
  - Safety warnings (Voice Phishing, Drugs, Electric Scooter rules).
- **Map:** Interactive map showing the location of Pai Chai University International Office and Daejeon Immigration Office.
